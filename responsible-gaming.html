What Is Responsible Gambling?

Play With Spare Money Only

Never use money set aside for necessities such as rent, utilities, food, or important personal responsibilities.

Control Your Play Time

Set a fixed amount of time before you start playing and make sure you stop when that time is reached.

Learn the Game First

Always understand how the game works and know the odds before placing any bets. The casino always has a mathematical edge.

Don’t Try to Win Back Losses

Losing is part of gambling. Chasing losses usually leads to even bigger financial and emotional stress.

Take Regular Breaks

Step away from gambling regularly to stay focused, avoid fatigue, and maintain full control of your decisions.

Know When to Stop

If gambling stops being enjoyable or starts causing stress, it’s a sign to pause and seek support if needed.

Gambling can be habit-forming, so it’s important to play responsibly and never risk more than you can afford to lose. If you feel that gambling may be becoming a problem, please seek help and support without hesitation.

18+ Only - Gambling in the UK is strictly limited to those aged 18 and over. Underage gambling is illegal and may lead to serious harm.
